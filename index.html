About the Documentary

The ecosystem has been drastically altered by human actions like deforestation, pollution, urbanization, and industrialization. The EcoLens documentary hopes to expose such changes, presenting actual effects on ecosystems, wildlife, and communities. The documentary hopes to draw viewers in through passionate storytelling, facts from experts, and solutions towards sustainable lifestyles.
